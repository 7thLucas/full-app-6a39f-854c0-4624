# Product Overview

## Product Name
Hello Orias

## Product Type
Simple personal landing page

## Description
A single-section landing page with one clear message: "Hello Orias." Minimal, clean, and direct — one section, no navigation, no clutter, no distractions. The page exists to deliver one greeting and nothing else.

## Target Users
Personal use — anyone wanting a clean, instant web presence anchored by a single greeting.

## Positioning
The anti-complexity landing page. Zero noise. One message. Instantly live.

## Brand & Tone
Calm, minimal, confident. Editorial simplicity — bold type does the work, white space does the rest.

## Scope
- 1 section (full-screen hero)
- Text: "Hello Orias"
- Clean typography, no extra elements

## Strategic Principles
- Less is more: the page's entire value is its restraint
- Instant clarity: message is understood within one second of landing
- Zero maintenance: nothing to break, nothing to update
